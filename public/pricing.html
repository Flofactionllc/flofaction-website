Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between HRI Insurance and Flo Faction Insurance?

HRI Insurance handles Property & Casualty products (auto, home, business) under a 20-44 license, while Flo Faction Insurance handles life, health, Medicare, and disability insurance products.

Can I upgrade my tier later?

Yes! You can upgrade your service tier at any time. Your existing data and history will be preserved, and you'll gain immediate access to additional features.

What does the AI-powered instant quotes include?

Our AI analyzes your information in real-time to provide accurate quotes from multiple carriers within seconds, along with personalized recommendations based on your profile.

What is a dedicated AI agent?

Elite tier members get their own named AI assistant that learns your preferences, remembers your history, and provides personalized service 24/7 across all communication channels.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No long-term contracts required. You can change or cancel your service tier at any time. Enterprise clients may have custom arrangements.

How does the Enterprise tier work?

Enterprise is customized for businesses. It includes full SaaS platform access, API integration, white-label options, and dedicated support. Contact us for custom pricing.
